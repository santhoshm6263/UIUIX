Case Study

The journey from concept to completion.

1

Problem Statement

Users were overwhelmed by complex data interfaces. The goal was to simplify without removing core functionalities.

2

Research & Persona

Interviewed 50+ users. Identified core pain points: cluttered navigation, poor readability, and lack of visual hierarchy.

3

Wireframes

Iterated through multiple low-fidelity wireframes to establish the optimal layout and user journey.

4

Final Design

Applied the futuristic glassmorphism style, modern typography, and glowing accents to the tested wireframes.
